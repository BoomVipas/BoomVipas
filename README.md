### About

AI engineer with production experience across the full LLM stack. I solo-engineer **[Callme TH](https://github.com/BoomVipas/callme-server)**, a Thai-language voice AI receptionist — Thai STT → multi-provider LLM orchestration (OpenAI, Gemini, Claude) → TTS, with a RAG/vector-DB layer for brand-specific FAQ handling and a guardrail framework to keep live phone calls hallucination-free. It integrates Google Calendar for real-time booking and LINE for post-call owner summaries.

Previously: data science intern at **GPSC**, building an automated Thai invoice OCR pipeline on Gemini AI + Google Cloud (93.6% accuracy); Product Lead at **Wander**, a Thailand travel app that won the NIA Startup Thailand League university round. IEEE paper submitted for **PILLo**, an AI-IoT medication dispensing system.

- 🔭 Currently building: **Callme TH** — Thai AI receptionist, AIaaS
- 🎓 B.Eng Robotics & AI, Chulalongkorn University (2022–2026)
- 🌱 Exploring: streaming voice pipelines, low-latency turn-taking, agent guardrails
